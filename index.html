About Me

Hi. I'm Angela.

I am a multi-disciplinary designer who is inspired
to create a better way for users to experience
digital media. I love creative problem solving,
visual storytelling, and brainstorming with others.
I have experience in animation, video editing,
production and graphic design. I have been
working professionally as a designer and art
director for over 8 years.

When I’m not designing, I like drawing and
animating, playing with my dog, watching movies
and writing/recording music with my band.

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out
to me, I’d love to hear from you!
